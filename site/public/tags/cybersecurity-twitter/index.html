Malicious Twitter Usage: Repurposing Twitter Accounts

Twitter accounts are frequency sold, contrary to Twitter Usage Policy which dictates that this practice is not allowed. Using historical data of Twitter accounts, accounts which were repurposed were identified and provided as examples and proof to this practice. The malicious nature of these sales were also cited. I worked as a part of a two person team to complete this project. (Full Paper)
